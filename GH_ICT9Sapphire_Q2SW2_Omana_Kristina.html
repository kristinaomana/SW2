Dog Breeds


Golden Retriever		 The Golden retriever is famous for its beautiful gold fur. This dog breed has a broad head, friendly and intelligent eyes, short ears, and a straight muzzle. Their life expetancy is 10-12 years.

Shih Tzu		 The Shih Tzu is a longhaired, active and alert dog. They originated from Tibet and are a very playful dog breed. They have short legs and do not require much exercise. They need daily brushing for their grooming.

German Shepherd		 German Shepherds are large and muscular dogs. They can be gentle family pets and also steadfast guardians. Most German Shepherds are healthy dogs. They are highly intelligent and a very athletic breed.
